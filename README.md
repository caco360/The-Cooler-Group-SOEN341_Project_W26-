Being a student can be so time consuming that you may lose track of your dietary needs. If you are a student who has trouble planning meals, tracking groceries or simply searching for new easy recipees, then this app is for you! 

MealMajor is a one-stop-shop for students to be able to meal-prep, track your meals and more!

Take advantage of everything feature MealMajor has to offer such as:
- Creating an account which will allow you to enter all your diet preferences and allergies.
- Our personalised recipe manager that allows you to create,  edit and delete your recipes. You can also add all kinds of details to your recipee such as ingredients, preperation time, preparation steps, cost and more. (features to come)
- Search for your recipes
- Filter your recipes based off of time, difficulty, cost, dietary tags and more (features to come)
- the Weekly Meal Planner allows you to create a weekly meal plan viewable in a weekly grid
- You can assign recipes to different days of the week and by meal types such as breakfast, dinner, snack, etc
- you'll be able to edit or remove meals from the planner at any time
- You'll also be able to prevent duplicates for the same week
